Our Story

Arockia Medical Centre, Emergency & Trauma Care is a trusted Multi-Speciality Hospital located on Bhavani Main Road, Kavindapadi, Erode. We are dedicated to providing compassionate, affordable, and advanced medical care to our community and surrounding rural areas.

Our hospital is equipped with a 24/7 Operation Theatre, Modular OT, in-house lab, digital X-ray, 24/7 pharmacy, and staffed by experienced specialists across Orthopedics, OG, General Medicine, Diabetic Wound Care, Pediatrics, and more.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, affordable, and advanced medical care with compassion and commitment to every patient — especially those in rural and underserved communities.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted hospital in the Erode region, setting new standards in patient care and clinical outcomes.

Our Community

Trusted by Kavindapadi, Bhavani, and surrounding villages for quality rural healthcare.
