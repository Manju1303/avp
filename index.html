About Arockia Medical Centre

Our Story

Arockia Medical Centre & Hospital is a trusted multi-specialty healthcare facility located on Bhavani Main Road, Kavindapadi, Erode. We are dedicated to providing compassionate, affordable, and advanced medical care to our community.

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and surgical facilities, staffed by a team of highly qualified specialists across Urology, Pediatrics, Orthopaedics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Pulmonology, and General Medicine.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class healthcare accessible to all, with empathy and integrity at the core of every interaction.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in the Erode region, setting new standards in patient care and clinical outcomes.
